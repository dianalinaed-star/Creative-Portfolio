About

The Story
Behind the Work

Hi, I'm Diana Lina — a creative strategist and designer with a passion for turning ideas into something people can actually feel and connect with.

My journey started in social services after earning my Psychology degree from Queens College. While working as an outreach coordinator, I saw an opportunity to bring our work into the digital space. I created a Social Media Committee, building strategies across departments to help the organization better communicate its message. That experience shaped how I think about design — not just as something visual, but as a tool for connection and impact.

Over time, I've grown my work across nonprofits, small businesses, and cultural spaces — leading campaigns, designing for large-scale events, and creating moments that live both digitally and in real life. More recently, my work has expanded into AI and tech-driven spaces, where I focus on translating complex ideas into clear, engaging narratives through pitch decks and presentations.

At the core of everything I do is storytelling. Whether I'm working on a campaign, a brand, or a pitch, I care about making things that resonate. Born and raised in New York City — where most of my work took place — I'm newly based in Maryland and excited to bring that same energy to new communities and collaborations.
